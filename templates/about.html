Education

I am currently a student at Kirkwood Community College. I am working towards my Associates in Science in Computer Software Development. For seven years, I taught English as a Second Language. I have a bachelors of art in English and Linguistics. I also have a Masters degree in Education in Curriculum and Instruction.

Philosophy of Education

Learning requires a holistic, communicative approach where students construct knowledge by means of personal and social interactions. Learning is built upon prior knowledge that and must be taught in a way that the student can practically use. New skills and knowledge are gained via crafting meaningful experiences where the students are central.

Experiences Overseas

I lived in Korea for two years. During that time, I taught elementary school English as a second language courses as well as teacher professional development courses. I was teacher of the year in my district in 2009. I also got the opportunity to travel to Thailand, Cambodia, Vietnam, Japan, and the Philippines while there.
